void lemons::gui::scale ( juce::Component &  component,
const juce::Rectangle< float > &  boundsRatio 
)

Scales a component relatively to the bounds of its parent.

The ratios are provided in the form of a rectangle, ie {x, y, w, h}.

Parameters
componentThe component to scale. This component must have a parent assigned.
boundsRatioThe ratio to the parent component's bounds to which the child will be scaled. If you pass {0.f, 0.f, 1.f, 1.f}, the scaled component will take up the entire area of its parent. If you pass {0.5f, 0.5f, 0.5f, 0.5f}, the scaled component will be half the area of the parent and will sit in the lower right corner of the parent.
